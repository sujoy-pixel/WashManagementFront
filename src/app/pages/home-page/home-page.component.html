About Us

Masco School Kanchan is a modern, Cambridge Curriculum English Medium School (CAIE) providing Pre-school and Primary education in a sprawling green campus at Kanchan. The school is equipped with all state-of-the-art facilities required for nurturing its students' talents and providing an international standard education & mentoring through experienced teachers and academic counselors. You are welcome to visit our campus & explore more about us!
